# Privacy Policy for Habit Tracker

**Last Updated: November 23, 2025**

## Introduction

Son Software ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Habit Tracker mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What We Collect
Habit Tracker is designed with privacy in mind. The app:
- Does NOT collect any personal information
- Does NOT require user accounts or registration
- Does NOT transmit any data to external servers
- Does NOT track user behavior or analytics

### Local Data Storage
All data created in the app is stored locally on your device only:
- Habit names and icons you create
- Completion dates and streaks
- All statistics and progress data

This data:
- Remains on your device
- Is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Third-Party Services

Habit Tracker does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. All data stays on the user's device.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, your data security depends on your device's security settings. We recommend:
- Using device passcode/biometric authentication
- Keeping your device's operating system updated
- Using device backup features (iCloud) if you want to preserve your data

## Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- All data is stored locally on your device
- You can delete the app at any time to remove all data
- No data is retained by us after app deletion

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
